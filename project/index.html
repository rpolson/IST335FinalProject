Who are we?

Medea is a chemical controls company based out of Akron, Ohio. With well over 300 employees both in office and on site, we are dedicated to identifying, fixing, and monitoring any issues you may be having with your plant or factory. Through our work everyday we seek to further the advances of the STEM field while providing the best possible service to our partners and clients. By choosing to do business with us you are guaranteeing the most effective and innovative solutions to keep your plant functioning at maximum peak performance.

What do we do?

We specialize in 4 services. With our partnership with Emerson we will be able to provide a wide variety of valves, actuators, and regulators to help build/maintain your production line. With a wide selection of measurement tools and analytical technologies we will be able to help you monitor what is happening at your site. Our automation and control staff will be able to safely operate and optimize your plants production while also providing recipe writing and oversight of the health of your production line. Your products and services will always be top quality with our lifecycle services team that is responsible for making sure everything is running on time and repeating precisely the proper steps in the production process to ensure quality end products.
